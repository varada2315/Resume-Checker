Upgrade Optosaur to follow a "Strict but Improvement-Oriented" evaluation model.

The system must:
- Be realistic and honest in scoring.
- Penalize genuine gaps.
- Clearly explain why points were deducted.
- Provide actionable improvements for each deduction.

---------------------------------------
CORE PHILOSOPHY
---------------------------------------

Optosaur behaves like a strict recruiter who:
- Identifies real gaps.
- Does not inflate scores.
- Explains deductions transparently.
- Provides practical improvement advice.

---------------------------------------
STEP 1: JD STRUCTURED EXTRACTION
---------------------------------------

From the Job Description extract:

1) Mandatory Skills (hard requirements)
2) Domain Keywords (industry-specific terms)
3) Required Years of Experience
4) Role Type (PM, Developer, Data, etc.)
5) Core Responsibilities
6) Tools/Tech Requirements

Classify mandatory skills if:
- Phrases like "must have", "required", "3–5 years", "experience in"
- Or repeatedly mentioned domain keywords

Return structured JSON.

---------------------------------------
STEP 2: RESUME STRUCTURED EXTRACTION
---------------------------------------

Extract from resume:

- Skills list
- Tools list
- Domain keywords present
- Years of experience (estimate from dates)
- Internship vs full-time differentiation
- Number of quantified bullet points
- Product lifecycle ownership indicators
- Presence of required sections

Return structured JSON.

---------------------------------------
STEP 3: HONEST WEIGHTED SCORING
---------------------------------------

Use this scoring model:

Mandatory Skill Match – 30%
Domain Relevance – 20%
Experience Alignment – 20%
Responsibility Alignment – 15%
Quantified Impact – 10%
ATS Structure – 5%

Rules:

1) If more than 40% of mandatory skills missing:
   Cap final score at 65.

2) If domain keywords absent:
   Deduct 10–15%.

3) If experience below required range:
   Deduct proportionally.

4) Structure compliance cannot increase score beyond 5%.

5) Final score must strictly align with numeric deductions.

Return:
- Final score
- Category breakdown
- Explicit deduction explanations

Example deduction format:
-10% Domain gap: No KYC/Fintech experience detected.
-8% Experience gap: Required 3–5 years, detected ~1 year internship.
-6% Low quantified impact: Only 1 measurable bullet found.

---------------------------------------
STEP 4: IMPROVEMENT-ORIENTED AI LAYER
---------------------------------------

Send structured gap analysis + resume text to OpenAI.

AI must:

1) Explain major gaps honestly.
2) Clarify which gaps are fixable short-term vs long-term.
3) Rewrite weak bullets ONLY in context of JD.
4) Suggest skill additions ONLY if realistically learnable.
5) Avoid hallucinating experience.
6) Not inflate profile beyond truth.

Add instruction:
"If core domain experience is missing, state clearly that it is a structural gap and cannot be solved purely by rewriting."

---------------------------------------
STEP 5: VERDICT LOGIC
---------------------------------------

Score > 80:
Highly Likely to Shortlist

Score 65–80:
Competitive but Needs Strengthening

Score 50–65:
Borderline – Domain or Experience Gap

Score < 50:
Low Match – Significant Misalignment

Verdict must strictly follow numeric score.
