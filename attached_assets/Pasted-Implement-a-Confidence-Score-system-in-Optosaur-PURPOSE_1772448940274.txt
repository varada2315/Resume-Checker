Implement a Confidence Score system in Optosaur.

PURPOSE:
Confidence Score measures how reliable the evaluation is,
NOT how good the resume is.

----------------------------------------
STEP 1: DEFINE CONFIDENCE COMPONENTS
----------------------------------------

Calculate confidence from:

1) JD Parsing Completeness (0–20)
   - Were required skills extracted?
   - Were responsibilities extracted?
   - Was experience requirement detected?

2) Resume Parsing Completeness (0–20)
   - Skills detected?
   - Experience years detected?
   - Bullet structure detected?

3) Keyword Mapping Reliability (0–15)
   - % of JD requirements successfully mapped to resume analysis.

4) Experience Detection Certainty (0–15)
   - Clear date ranges?
   - Or inferred estimates?

5) Structural Data Strength (0–10)
   - Quantified bullets detected?
   - Clear sections?

6) Validation Integrity (0–20)
   - No hallucination rejection triggers?
   - No rewrite sanitization failures?

----------------------------------------
STEP 2: CALCULATE FINAL CONFIDENCE
----------------------------------------

confidence_score = sum(all components)
Max = 100

----------------------------------------
STEP 3: DISPLAY IN UI
----------------------------------------

Display below Match Score:

Confidence Score: 84%
Label:
- High Reliability
- Good Reliability
- Moderate Reliability
- Limited Reliability

Add tooltip:

"This score reflects how confidently Optosaur was able to evaluate your resume based on structured data extraction and requirement clarity."

----------------------------------------
STEP 4: LOW CONFIDENCE HANDLING
----------------------------------------

If confidence < 65%:
Show suggestion:

"Improve resume formatting and clarity to increase evaluation accuracy."

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT
----------------------------------------

Confidence Score must NOT depend on match score.
It must depend only on analysis reliability.
